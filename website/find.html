Directions
The Church can be found at the junction of Severn Road and Eden Drive. Both can be accessed via Greenhills Road.

The Church building is set a short distance away from this main road and can be found very near Mossneuk Primary School Community Car Park and the local ScotMid Co-op shop.


Facilites

  • Off-Street Car Parking in a Private Carpark
  • Disabled Access & Wheelchair friendly
  • Hearing induction loop system
  • Male, Female & Disabled toilet facilities
